About me

I'm passionate about reinforcement learning, robotics, control systems and optimization theory. I completed my B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee with CGPA of 9.3/10, where I built a strong foundation in both theory and practical applications. Currently, I work as a Staff Controls Engineer at Enphase Energy, where I focus on developing and refining control strategies for both new and existing energy products.

I have prior research experience from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, where I worked on image processing algorithms to train an Unmanned Surface Vehicle (USV) for autonomous task execution.

Outside of work, I stay active by playing badminton, volleyball, and engaging in regular workouts. When I'm not on the court or in the gym, you'll often find me playing the flute, gaming, reading books, or watching movies.

I enjoy turning research ideas into practical applications and sharing my implementation experiences through articles. I value clean, readable, and self-contained code—Python is my language of choice.
